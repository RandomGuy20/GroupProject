About Circuit Solutions

At Circuit Solutions we are passionate about all things tech. Based in Orlando we specialize in providing comprehensive hardware repairs, custom PC builds, and all the latest products to our valued customers.

Our team of skilled technicians and tech enthusiasts are dedicated to delivering exceptional service and quality workmanship. Whethere you're looking to repair a device, build a custom rig, or shop for the newest tech, we have you covered.

We pride ourselves on our commitemnt to customer satisfaction, innovation, and excellence. Join us in exploring the world of technology - where your tech needs are met with expertise and a personal touch.

Our Story

Circuit Solutions was dounded by a couple fo tech enthusiasts who noticed a significant gap in reliable tech support in Orlando. We saw an opportunity to create a space where anyone, from elders needing a helping hand with their devices to the younger generation passionate about RGB setups and gaming gear, could find the support they needed.

By placing ourselves in the heart of Orlando we made it our mission to be easily accessible to all. We pride ourselves on being a welcoming hub for tech users of all ages and skill levels.

We also are proud to privide expert solutions with a personal touch. Our journey began with a simpl goal: to be the go-to tech resource for our community. We always continue to evolve, always putting our customers first.
